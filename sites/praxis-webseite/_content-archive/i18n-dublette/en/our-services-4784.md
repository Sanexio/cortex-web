<h2>Internal medicine and primary care</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading --><!-- wp:image {"id":265,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","translatedWithWPMLTM":"1"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://gpmedicalcenterwestend-7ded2f4ae8c4343d2029-202604.local/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/Teambild_m.jpeg" alt="" class="wp-image-265"/><figcaption>The medical team</figcaption></figure><!-- /wp:image --><!-- wp:paragraph {"translatedWithWPMLTM":"1"} -->
<p>In our practice we offer internal diagnostics which can be quickly completed in all areas in cooperation with our network of specialists.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --><!-- wp:paragraph {"translatedWithWPMLTM":"1"} -->
<p>Since prevention is always better than cure, we also offer you an important preventive program. We cover a spectrum for which you would otherwise have to visit several doctors in different places.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --><!-- wp:paragraph {"translatedWithWPMLTM":"1"} -->
<p>We are well networked in Frankfurt and can therefore arrange a quick appointment with our specialist colleagues for any further examinations required, for example in the fields of orthopedics, gynecology, urology, ENT, dermatology or ophthalmology.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For our international patients, we also offer medical consultations in various languages, in particular in English, French or Spanish.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --><!-- wp:paragraph {"translatedWithWPMLTM":"1"} -->
<p>During our daily open consultation hours between 11:00 and 12:00 you have the opportunity to present yourself without an appointment in the case of acute complaints.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --><!-- wp:paragraph {"translatedWithWPMLTM":"1"} -->
<p>We know that for many of our patients, the lost waiting time on the way to the practice and in the waiting room can be more expensive than the treatment itself. For this reason, we attach importance to efficient processes in our practice so that you do not have to wait.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
